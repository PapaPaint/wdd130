Content

Brainstorm

Why will people come to your site?

People will come to my website because they are interested in building or repairing their computers, or they are seeking guidance on building their dream computer. As a Computer Technician and Custom PC Specialist, my website serves as a platform to showcase my expertise in repairing computers and building custom PC rigs. Visitors will be drawn to my site because they are looking for reliable information, expert advice, and solutions to their computer-related problems. Whether they are DIY enthusiasts, gamers, or professionals in need of specialized computer setups, my website offers valuable insights and services tailored to their needs.

What questions will your visitors expect your site to answer for them?

Visitors to my site will expect answers to various questions related to building and repairing computers, such as:

1. How can I build a custom PC that meets my specific requirements?

2. What components do I need to consider when building a gaming PC?

3. How can I troubleshoot common computer problems on my own?

4. What tools and techniques are essential for computer repair?

5. How can I optimize the performance of my computer?

6. What are the latest trends and advancements in PC hardware?

7. How can I upgrade my existing computer to enhance its performance?

8. Are there any maintenance tips I should follow to prolong the lifespan of my computer?

9. Where can I find reliable resources and tutorials for computer building and repair?

What types of activities will visitors want to complete on your site?

Visitors to my site will likely want to engage in the following activities:

1. Reading informative articles and guides on computer building, repair, and maintenance.

2. Watching video tutorials demonstrating step-by-step processes for building and repairing computers.

3. Participating in forums or discussion boards to seek advice from experts and fellow enthusiasts.

4. Accessing a comprehensive database of computer components and accessories to make informed purchasing decisions.

5. Booking appointments for professional computer repair services or customized PC builds.

6. Subscribing to newsletters or email updates to stay informed about the latest industry news and trends.

7. Connecting with other users through social media platforms to share experiences and tips.

What information is necessary to complete any actions your site offers?

To complete actions such as booking appointments for repair services or custom PC builds, visitors will need to provide essential information such as their contact details, location, description of the issue or requirements, preferred date and time for the service, and any additional specifications or preferences they may have. This information will enable me to understand their needs better and provide tailored solutions and services accordingly.

Why will your visitors come to your site instead of another to get this information or complete the actions?

Visitors will choose my site over others for several reasons:

1. Expertise and Experience: As a Computer Technician and Custom PC Specialist, I offer specialized knowledge and hands-on experience in building and repairing computers, ensuring that visitors receive accurate and reliable information and services.

2. Customized Solutions: My website provides personalized recommendations and solutions tailored to each visitor's specific requirements and preferences, whether they are looking to build a gaming PC, upgrade their existing setup, or troubleshoot technical issues.

3. Comprehensive Resources: My site offers a wide range of resources, including articles, guides, tutorials, and forums, covering various aspects of computer building, repair, and maintenance, making it a one-stop destination for all their computer-related needs.

4. Quality of Service: Visitors can expect top-notch customer service, timely responses to inquiries, and professional assistance throughout their interaction with my site, ensuring a positive and satisfying experience.

5. Community Engagement: My website fosters a sense of community by encouraging users to interact with each other, share their experiences, and seek advice, creating a supportive and collaborative environment for computer enthusiasts and beginners alike.

Wireframes

Create three wireframes for your site. One for each page and list them here

Home

Include a prominent call-to-action (CTA) button or section encouraging visitors to contact or book your services. Consider featuring customer testimonials or success stories to build trust and credibility. Showcase any special offers, discounts, or promotions to attract potential customers.

Services

Provide detailed descriptions of the services offered, including computer repairs, custom PC builds, and any additional services like software installation or upgrades. Include pricing information or a pricing estimator tool to give visitors an idea of costs. Highlight any certifications, qualifications, or industry affiliations to establish credibility and expertise.

Resources

Ensure the troubleshooting guides cover a wide range of common computer issues and provide clear, step-by-step instructions. Include visuals such as diagrams or video tutorials to enhance understanding, especially in the PC building resources section. Make the customer support section easily accessible with multiple contact options, including phone, email, and live chat. Develop a comprehensive FAQ section addressing common inquiries about your services, pricing, warranty, etc.
